Every new version of Firefox Come with new features. Recently people using Firefox 21 Reported that Their Firefox has crashed after click on X-close button.

What is the problem ?

Actually Firefox have trouble with “hardware acceleration”. You need to disable it. Try to go to Tools > options > Browsing > uncheck the hardware acceleration. This is one of the problem in Firefox.

If you have any kind of trouble then I recommended you to try https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/questions/new for get official help & support For Firefox
